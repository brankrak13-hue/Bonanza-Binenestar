# **App Name**: Bonanza Parfum

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a comprehensive catalog of perfumes, makeup, skincare, and other luxury items.
- Product Detail Pages: Detailed information, high-quality images, and pricing for individual products.
- Image Sharpening: Use generative AI to sharpen user-uploaded pictures to enhance the details in images of products
- Shopping Cart: A standard shopping cart feature to add, remove, and manage products for purchase.
- User Accounts: Allow users to create and manage accounts, save addresses, and track order history.
- Newsletter Subscription: Implement a newsletter subscription form to collect user emails for marketing purposes.
- Contact Form: A contact form for users to reach out with questions, concerns, or feedback.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#C9A84C) to evoke luxury and sophistication.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) for a clean and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Black (#000000) for strong contrast and emphasis.
- Headline font: 'Cormorant Garamond' serif for titles to convey elegance and tradition.
- Body font: 'Montserrat' sans-serif for readability in descriptions and body text.
- Use minimalist, elegant icons in gold or black to maintain a luxury feel.
- Employ a clean, spacious layout with plenty of white space to highlight products and imagery.
